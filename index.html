Come roll with us!

The Roleplay cafe is a home for all us roleplayers. With us you can find new games to join, host your own campaign or find out what roleplaying is all about!

Next Event: DCC Funnel: The Portal Under the Stars

Our next upcoming event is a Dungeon Crawl Classics funnel adventure: The Portal Under the Stars. Roll up a bunch of level 0 peasants and explore a dangerous dungeon. Many might perish, but those who survive will turn into heroes! Suitable for new players, as the rules for level 0 characters are very basic.

Level up your roleplaying experience with our drinks and food!

We have a great selection of drinks and food to accompany your roleplaying adventures. Doing a fantasy dungeon crawl? Try a Guinness West Indies Porter with a plate of our spicy chicken drumsticks. Roleplaying as cyberpunks on the neon streets of a future mega-city? Sip on a Black Russian while munching on a Future Fusion Burger!
