Why visit if you are a player?

It can be hard finding people to play roleplaying games with. We make it easier for you!

  • No more worrying about who hosts the game: we provide the space.
  • No more worrying about food: we provide the food.
  • Looking to learn about a new game? We host many different games.

Upcoming events

Dungeon Crawl Classics funnel: The Portal Under the Stars

Roll up a bunch of level 0 peasants and explore a dangerous dungeon. Many might perish, but survivors will become heroes! Suitable for new players, as the rules for level 0 characters are very simple.

Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition: The West Marches

Join this ongoing The West Marches game. This is a sandbox game, where players get to decide themselves which adventure to go on. They tell the gamemaster what they want to do, and the gamemaster prepares their adventure.

Call of Cthulhu: The Haunting

This is an introductory adventure for the Call of Cthulhu roleplaying game. You play as investigators in 1920s America. Can you discover the mystery behind the haunting of the Corbitt House?

Why visit if you're a gamemaster?

Taking your game to the next level can be hard, and expensive. Minis, terrain, sound system... not everybody can afford all of that, or has room for all of that. We can take care of that for you:

  • Want minis? We have them! Still missing certain minis? Request them!
  • Need terrain? We have it! Still missing terrain pieces? Request them!
  • We also provice sound systems for gamemasters looking to add music or audio effects.
  • We also provide beamers for gamemasters looking to add computer maps to their game.

Submit your game idea

We're always looking for gamemasters who want to run their games at the Roleplay Cafe. If you have an idea for an adventure or a campaign, let us know using the form below. Give us a brief pitch and mention the gamesystem you intend to use. We try to make sure all kinds of roleplaying games are being played, so it could be that we put you on a waiting list.

New to roleplaying games?

So you've heard about this thing called roleplaying games and you want to have fictional adventures with your friends? Well then, you're in luck! Roleplaying games are going through a bit of a renaissance right now: the combination of a new edition of Dungeons and Dragons and the rise of streaming on platforms like Twitch has created a perfect storm. Which is why the Roleplay Cafe was opened: to spread the joy of roleplaying games to more people. So come to the Roleplay Cafe and join one of our newbie friendly games! One of our owners runs a game every two weeks that is perfectly suitable for new players: an evening of fun, action and adventure where all the players are goblins! We also have a weekly game of Dungeon Crawl Classics, where all the players create random peasants using a bunch of dice, and then get to send them through a dungeon to see which survive!
