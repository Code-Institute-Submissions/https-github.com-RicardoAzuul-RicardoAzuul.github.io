Why visit the Roleplay Cafe?

Players Gamemasters Newbies

Players

It can be hard finding people to play roleplaying games with. We make it easier for you!

  • No more worrying about who hosts the game: we provide the space.
  • No more worrying about food: we provide the food.
  • Looking to learn about a new game? We host many different games.
Upcoming events

Thursday 19:00 till about 23:00 - Dungeon Crawl Classics funnel: The Portal Under the Stars

Roll up a bunch of level 0 peasants and explore a dangerous dungeon. Many might perish, but survivors will become heroes! Suitable for new players, as the rules for level 0 characters are very simple.

Friday 19:00 till about 23:00 - Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition: The West Marches

Join this ongoing The West Marches game. This is a sandbox game, where players get to decide themselves which adventure to go on. They tell the gamemaster what they want to do, and the gamemaster prepares their adventure.

Saturday 20:00 till about 00:00 - Call of Cthulhu: The Haunting

This is an introductory adventure for the Call of Cthulhu roleplaying game. You play as investigators in 1920s America. Can you discover the mystery behind the haunting of the Corbitt House?

Gamemasters

Taking your game to the next level can be hard, and expensive. Minis, terrain, sound system... not everybody can afford all of that, or has room for all of that. We can take care of that for you:

  • Want minis? We have them! Still missing certain minis? Request them!
  • Need terrain? We have it! Still missing terrain pieces? Request them!
  • We also provice sound systems for gamemasters looking to add music or audio effects.
  • We also provide beamers for gamemasters looking to add digital maps to their game.

Newbies

So you've heard about this thing called roleplaying games and you want to have fun and adventures with your friends? You're in luck!

Join the community at the Roleplay Cafe and join one of our newbie friendly games!

Richard, one of our owners runs a game every other Tuesday that is perfectly suitable for new players: an evening of fun, action and adventure where all the players are goblins!

We also have a weekly game of Dungeon Crawl Classics every Thursday, where all the players quickly create characters at the table, and then get to send them through a dungeon to see who survives!
