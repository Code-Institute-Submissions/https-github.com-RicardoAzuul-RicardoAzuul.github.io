Directions

By car

Amsterdam is not very car friendly, but if you insist: the closest parking garage is Parkeergarage De Hallen, at Bellamyplein 41, 1053 AT Amsterdam. From there it's a 5 minute walk to get to the Roleplay Cafe!

Using public transport

From Amsterdam Central Station take tram 13 to Geuzenveld and get off at stop Willem de Zwijgerlaan (the 6th stop). From there it's a 2 minute walk. This should take about 20 minutes!

From Amsterdam Sloterdijk Station take tram 19 to Diemen Sniep and get off at stop Willem de Zwijgerlaan (the 7th stop). From there it's a 3 minute walk. This should take about 20 minutes!

In order to reliably plan your public transport route, use the official site of the public transport company of Amsterdam: GVB
